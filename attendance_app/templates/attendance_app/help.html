Smart Attendance System User Guide

This guide will help you understand how to use our QR-based attendance system effectively.

For Students

How to Mark Attendance

  1. Log in to your student account.
  2. Ask your teacher for the session ID or QR code.
  3. Go to the "Mark Attendance" page for that session.
  4. Scan the QR code displayed by your teacher or enter the code manually.
  5. Submit the form to mark your attendance.

View Your Attendance

  1. Go to your dashboard to see your overall attendance.
  2. Click on "Attendance Summary" for detailed course-wise information.
  3. Monitor your attendance percentage to ensure it stays above 75%.

For Teachers

Create a New Session

  1. Log in to your teacher account.
  2. Click on "Create Session" in the dashboard.
  3. Fill in the course, date, time, and room details.
  4. Submit to create a new session with a unique QR code.

Display QR Code for Students

  1. From your dashboard, find the session you want to take attendance for.
  2. Click "Generate QR" to display the QR code.
  3. Show this code to students or project it in the classroom.
  4. The code will expire after 15 minutes for security.

View & Export Attendance Reports

  1. Click "View Report" for any session to see who attended.
  2. Use the "Export CSV" button to download the data for your records.

For Administrators

System Management

  1. Access the admin dashboard to view system-wide statistics.
  2. Review attendance reports for all sessions.
  3. Access detailed reports to monitor attendance patterns.
